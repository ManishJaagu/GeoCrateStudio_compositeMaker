GeoCrate Studio is a non-profit, open-source platform that allows users to generate RGB composites from satellite imagery datasets such as Landsat, Sentinel, and Indian Remote Sensing satellites like the Resourcesat Series. Users can generate color composites either automatically or manually without performing complex processing steps. Whether you're a researcher, student, or enthusiast, GeoCrate Studio simplifies the task of visualizing satellite data into intuitive and accessible workflows.

Note: Automation features for additional satellites will be available in future updates.
